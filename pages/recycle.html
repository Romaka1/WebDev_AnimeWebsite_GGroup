Our 3-Stage Recycling Process

Stage 1: Collection

The first step in our recycling process begins with you! Customers can bring their used hoodies, manga books, and other items to our designated collection points in stores across Ireland. This stage helps us gather unwanted items that would otherwise end up in landfills, contributing to waste reduction.

Stage 2: Transportation

Once collected, the items are carefully sorted and prepared for transport. From Ireland, they are shipped to specialized recycling facilities across the European Union. We prioritize eco-friendly transportation methods to minimize our carbon footprint during this stage.

Stage 3: Recycling in EU Factories

The final stage takes place in state-of-the-art recycling factories located in the EU. Here, the items are broken down into raw materials, such as fibers for hoodies or paper pulp for manga books. These materials are then processed and transformed into brand-new, sustainable products. This approach ensures a closed-loop system, reducing waste and promoting a greener planet.
